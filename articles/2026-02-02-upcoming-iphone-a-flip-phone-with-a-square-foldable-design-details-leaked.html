Upcoming iPhone: A "Flip" Phone With a Square Foldable Design, Details Leaked

Open source (Reddit) • Published 2026-02-02T01:00:32+00:00

The smartphone world is buzzing with a wild new rumor: Apple might be working on a foldable iPhone that flips shut like a classic clamshell, but with a distinctly modern, square profile.

The Leaked Details

According to a recent leak sourced from a Reddit discussion, Apple's long-rumored foray into foldables could take the form of a flip phone. The most striking detail is the alleged "square" design when folded, suggesting a more compact, almost pocket-square form factor compared to the longer, narrower designs of current flip phones like the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip series. This leak, which appears to be based on supply chain or prototype information, describes a device that aims to merge nostalgia with cutting-edge foldable display technology.

It's crucial to note that this is an unverified leak, not an official announcement from Apple. The company's plans for a foldable device remain one of the tech industry's most tightly guarded secrets. The leak provides no concrete information on potential specs, release windows, or pricing. As with all early rumors, the final product—if it exists—could look very different.

Why This Matters

If true, this represents a monumental shift for Apple. The company has watched competitors like Samsung and Motorola iterate on foldable designs for years while maintaining a traditional slate form factor for the iPhone. A flip iPhone would signal Apple's belief that foldables are a mature and mainstream technology, worthy of its flagship brand. It would instantly become the most high-profile foldable device on the market, validating the entire category.

For consumers, the appeal is a blend of nostalgia and practicality. A flip design promises greater portability and protection for the main screen, addressing two common pain points of modern smartphones. The described square shape could offer a unique and potentially more ergonomic feel in the hand compared to existing models. The move would also introduce fierce new competition, likely accelerating innovation and potentially bringing down costs across the foldable market.

Key Takeaways

  • This is a major but unconfirmed leak; treat it as a strong rumor, not fact.
  • The described "square foldable design" would differentiate an iPhone flip from current market options.
  • An Apple foldable would be a watershed moment for the technology, pushing it further into the mainstream.
  • No details on launch timing, durability, or price are available—these are the critical unknowns.
  • Competition in the foldable space is about to get a lot more interesting if this comes to fruition.

While we wait for official word from Cupertino, the rumor mill is a great reminder of how fast mobile tech evolves. Keeping your current device in top shape is key, whether you're holding out for a folding future or just want to maximize your phone's lifespan today.

Speaking of getting the most out of your tech, ensuring your gadgets are protected is always a smart move. From robust cases to screen protectors, a little preparation can save you from headaches down the line, no matter what form factor your next phone takes.

The prospect of a flip iPhone is undeniably exciting, blending a beloved retro form with Apple's modern tech ethos. While we await concrete details, this leak has successfully shifted the conversation from "if" Apple will make a foldable to imagining just how they might reinvent the category.
