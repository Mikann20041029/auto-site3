The Hidden Reality

Stop the Damage / Secure the Win

  • Audit Your Dependencies: List every plugin, script, and custom workflow tied to Animate. Identify which are obsolete and which need replacement.

  • Communicate with Clients/Teams: Inform stakeholders of the impending change and potential timeline impacts for ongoing work.
  • Begin Skill Pivoting: Dedicate time now to learn the core features of your chosen replacement tool before the March 1st cutoff creates a panic.
